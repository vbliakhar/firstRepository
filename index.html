Product

First sharable air condition monitoring system. Use one application to monitor air quality at home, at office, in restaurant, on street, at your kids school.

  • Receive notifications when values are out of allowed ranges
  • Find open devices on map
  • Add private device by scanning QR code on box
  • Share devices with your friends and family
  • Receive recommendations regarding your district
  • Build a safe air community

Mission

In the current post-truth era, it is crucially important to provide everyone with a free access to a primary source of information whatever data is concerned. A basic right to know the actual quality conditions of the air we breathe is a must. Not only scientific organizations and huge corporations but every single earthling is to be able to monitor and control the quality of air at home, in office, throughout the city or elsewhere.


The AI-powered predictive analytics is what can deliver unbiased recommendations and precise forecasts regarding the measures capable of improving air quality conditions in our overpopulated urban areas. If obsolete industrial technologies cause the biggest air pollution, the contemporary digital technologies should help fix the situation and mitigate the climate change.
